Tuesday ☀️
However, the show must go on....
Wrapping up a couple of product / brand videos this week - both in the broad recruitment space, very different products but both pretty revolutionary.
And starting two new projects - neither of them in SaaS or tech.....Which is part of an interesting shift.
Four years ago, almost all the work we did was for software products. In the last 18 months, that's changed, we're doing a lot more for industries that never really touched motion or animation before: financial services, logistics, diagnostics, cyber security....
I think it's more than just different industries starting to use video though.
For a long time video felt like a top of funnel thing, get one company video made and that's the box ticked.
What we're seeing now is video showing up right through the process, product explainers, onboarding videos, educational content, reducing friction wherever someone's getting stuck rather than just making the first impression.
